# Good News Network - User Guide

## Welcome to Good News Network!

Good News Network (GNN) is your daily source for positive news from around the world. This guide will help you get started with the application.

## Getting Started

### 1. Creating an Account

When you first launch the app, you'll see the Welcome screen.

- Tap "Don't have an account? Sign Up" at the bottom
- Enter your full name
- Enter your email address
- Create a password
- Tap "Sign Up"

### 2. Signing In

If you already have an account:

- Enter your email address
- Enter your password
- Tap "Sign In"

## Main Features

### News Feed

The home screen displays a feed of positive news articles:

- **Scroll** through the feed to browse articles
- **Pull down** to refresh and get the latest news
- Each article shows:
  - News source
  - Article title
  - Brief summary
  - Publication date
- **Tap on any article** to open the full story in your browser

### Search

Find specific topics you're interested in:

- Use the search bar at the top of the home screen
- Type keywords related to the news you want to find
- Results update automatically as you type
- Clear the search to see all articles again

### Profile & Settings

Access your profile by tapping the "Profile" button:

- View your account information
- Toggle email notifications on/off
- Read about Good News Network
- Sign out when needed

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Stay Updated**: Pull down on the home screen regularly to refresh the news feed
2. **Discover Topics**: Use the search feature to explore specific subjects you care about
3. **Personalize**: Adjust your notification preferences in your profile

## Platform Availability

Good News Network works on:

- **Web**: Access from any modern web browser
- **iOS**: Download from the App Store (coming soon)
- **Android**: Download from Google Play Store (coming soon)
